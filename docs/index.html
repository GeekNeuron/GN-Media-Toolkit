GN Media Toolkit

An all-in-one command-line suite designed to manage, convert, and automate your entire media library.

About The Project

The GN Media Toolkit represents an evolution in media management—a shift from simple scripts to an integrated command philosophy. We abandoned the monolithic approach for a constellation of hyper-efficient, specialized tools. Each module is a master of its domain, engineered to perform its task with surgical precision, yet all work in harmony as part of a cohesive toolkit built on the power of FFmpeg, yt-dlp, and ImageMagick.

This arsenal includes the GN Master Downloader to automate your content acquisition, the GN Quality Enforcer to standardize your entire archive, the GN Scene Creator to generate cinematic previews, and the GN Ultimate Merger to combine any stream, alongside many more modules for compression, extraction, and analysis. This is not just a set of tools; it's your new command center for your media universe.

Explore the full project and its source code on the official GitHub repository.
